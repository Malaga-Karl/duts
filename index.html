Mission Statement

DUT shall be capable of advanced education in all fields of study that the university provides. It should also give the students the ability to serve the societal and technological change that the country needs, by application of the mindset, values, and competencies that they have fostered within the university.

Why D.U.T.S.?

Dioses University of Technology and Science (D.U.T.S.) is the first and only premier private university that has free tuition in the Philippines, as it is completely funded by its founder, Professor Raymund Dioses. DUTS is also the only university in the Philippines that has a 100% passing rate in midterms and finals exams of its students. As of 2022, it has also produced five students that are competent in the field of technology, and are active contributors to the advancement of the nation. The university has pledged itself to the development of its students with the objective of guiding them to become the future of the country's progress and industry.
